# Banana Smoothie

## Submitted by
[prahack](https://github.com/prahack)

## Ingredients
- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 cup milk (any type)
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4-5 ice cubes
- Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions
1. Peel and slice the bananas.
2. Add bananas, milk, yogurt, honey/maple syrup, and vanilla to a blender.
3. Add ice cubes.
4. Blend until smooth and creamy (about 30-60 seconds).
5. Pour into glasses and sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.
6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

## Preparation Time
5 minutes

## Difficulty
Easy

## Tags
Beverage, Breakfast, Snack, Vegetarian

## Notes
For a thicker smoothie, use frozen banana slices instead of fresh ones and reduce or eliminate the ice. You can also add a tablespoon of peanut butter or a handful of spinach for extra nutrition.
